About Me

I am passionate about the web.

I started taking Skillcrush classes after I moved from Texas to Missouri and found myself with nothing to occupy my time during the frigid winter months. I found that I loved front end web development, and have spent every spare moment since developing my skillset.

My focus is writing clean, well formatted, semantic HTML5 by hand to make sure the content is easy to read. You can trust me to take a designer's PSD and quickly & accurately convert it into a webpage that is a pixel-perfect match.
